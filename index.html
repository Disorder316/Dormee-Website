The Band

I am a classically trained Electronic Dance Music artist, producer, and Dj. I started formal training as a classical pianist and violinist at a young age and launched myself into the performance arena, performing numerous classical concerts throughout the Midwestern United States, winning competitions and performing with numerous orchestras as featured artist. I studied piano and violin performance at university but decided to drop my classical music career and create my own studio in 2014 as a collaborative EDM production studio working with local and international artists to be featured in my tracks. My style ranges from '90s House and Hip Hop to the grinding bass driven genres of Electro House and Dubstep.
